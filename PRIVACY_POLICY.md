# Privacy Policy for BucketList

**Last Updated: March 15, 2026**

## Introduction

BucketList ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Bucket list items**: Titles, descriptions, notes, and categories you create
- **Location data**: Addresses and coordinates you manually search for and add to your items
- **Friend information**: Names and usernames of friends you add to the app

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- We do **not** track your physical location or use GPS in the background
- We do **not** collect analytics or usage data
- We do **not** use cookies or tracking technologies
- We do **not** share any data with third parties

## Data Storage

All data created in BucketList is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data is:
- Stored only on your device
- Not uploaded to any server or cloud service
- Not accessible by us or any third party
- Protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)

## Third-Party Services

### Apple MapKit
The app uses Apple's MapKit framework to display maps and search for locations. When you search for a location, your search query is sent to Apple's servers. Apple's privacy policy applies to this data: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### Google Maps (Optional)
The app provides an option to open locations in Google Maps. This only occurs when you explicitly tap "Open in Google Maps." Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Sharing

BucketList does **not** share your data with any third parties. The "sharing" feature within the app currently operates locally on your device.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete individual items at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all app data from your device.

## Children's Privacy

BucketList does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- **Access**: All your data is visible within the app
- **Modify**: You can edit any item at any time
- **Delete**: You can delete any item or uninstall the app to remove all data
- **Export**: You can share items via the built-in share functionality

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
